The Social Sauna Movement

A European Heat Map of Opportunity

This is not a niche trend; it's a widespread cultural phenomenon. This map shows the explosive growth of social, wild, and mobile saunas across Europe. Each point represents a hub of activity, from bustling urban sauna communities to serene coastal retreats. Click the clusters to explore the hotspots.

Key Hubs of the Movement

United Kingdom & Ireland

The undeniable epicenter, with an explosion of over 180+ wild and community saunas, particularly along the south coast of England and a growing scene in Ireland.

Scandinavia & The Nordics

The cultural homeland. While sauna is traditional, the "wild sauna" model is thriving in scenic locations like the Norwegian fjords and along the Swedish coast.

Central & Eastern Europe

Grassroots movements are taking hold, with mobile sauna collectives in Berlin, a world-class sauna scene in Riga, and unique urban concepts in Prague.

The map shows a clear trend. Budapest is the next logical destination for this proven concept.
